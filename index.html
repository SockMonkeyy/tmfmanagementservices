What We Do

TMF Management Services provides comprehensive asset recovery for surplus, excess proceeds, and other unclaimed or undistributed funds. We analyze public and private records, verify entitlement, prepare and submit claims, and work directly with courts, counties, trustees, and holders to expedite release.

Types of Funds We Recover

Foreclosure Surplus

Proceeds remaining after a foreclosure sale once liens and costs are satisfied.

Tax Sale Excess Proceeds

Funds left after a tax deed or tax lien sale that often belong to the former owner.

Court Registry Funds

Interpleader and registry funds held by courts pending a valid claim.

Probate & Estate Funds

Undistributed or unclaimed funds owed to heirs or estates.

Trustee‑Held Funds

Proceeds held by trustees, servicers, or other third parties.

Other Unclaimed Funds

Additional categories requiring research and formal recovery.
