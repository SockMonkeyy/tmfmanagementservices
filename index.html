What Are Surplus & Excess Funds?

When a property is sold at a foreclosure auction or tax sale, it sometimes sells for more than the total amount owed on the mortgage, taxes, liens, and fees. The leftover money is called surplus funds or excess proceeds.

These funds usually belong to the former property owner — but they are not sent automatically. A proper claim must be filed.

Important: If you miss the deadline to claim these funds, you could permanently lose them.

Why Most People Never Recover Their Money
